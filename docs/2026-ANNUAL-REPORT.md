## Lessons Learned

1. **Archiving is not deleting** — 64 repos archived preserved history while cleaning the signal
2. **Documentation compounds** — one case study feeds LinkedIn, portfolio, and demos
3. **Structure over volume** — 20 excellent repos > 100 scattered projects
4. **Brand is built deliberately** — "Practical AI for Africa" is a choice, not an accident
5. **Relationships over followers** — 3 targeted collaborations > 10,000 passive followers
